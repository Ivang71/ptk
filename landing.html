Plain description of how the bot works

The Potok Bot in Telegram

The Potok Bot is a Telegram chat bot for people search. You send a short description of a role and company (for example, “Head of e‑commerce at XYZ” or “Director of Sales, Shopify”), and the bot looks for matching people on the open internet and returns likely full names and possible work emails.

  • ✓ Works as a normal Telegram chat, no extra applications or registrations are required.
  • ✓ You can write any combination of job title, company name and extra hints in free text.
  • ✓ For each query, the bot uses external search and language models to analyse public internet sources.
  • ✓ It responds with candidates: full name, role and one or two likely work email patterns based on the name and domain.

How a typical conversation works
  1. The user opens the bot in Telegram and writes what they know about the person: job position, company, and any other useful details. Step 1
  2. The bot sends the text to its backend, which prepares search queries and looks for relevant public information on the internet. Step 2
  3. Search results are analysed by language models that try to identify the most likely person or several candidates for the described role. Step 3
  4. The bot replies in the Telegram chat with a short summary: names, positions and possible work email addresses, or a message that nothing suitable was found. Step 4
Intended use and limitations
  • Intended for sales, business development, recruiting and similar teams that need a quick starting point for contacting potential clients or candidates. Intended use
  • The bot is an information and research helper. It does not make binding decisions or approvals and its output should be checked before use. Decision making
  • The bot does not ask for card numbers, passwords or other highly sensitive payment data and is not used to execute or confirm payments. Data it avoids
